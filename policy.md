# Privacy Policy for Voyage

**Last updated:** 30/01/2026

Voyage (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Voyage may collect the following categories of personal data:

### 1.1 Authentication Information
- Name  
- Email address  
- Profile picture  

Collected through **Google Sign-In using Firebase Authentication**.

### 1.2 User-Generated Content
- Travel itineraries and selected locations  
- Friendship requests and blocked users  
- Any other content voluntarily created by the user within the App  

### 1.3 Media Files
- Photos and attachments uploaded by the user  
Stored securely using **Firebase Storage**.

### 1.4 Usage and Technical Data
- Device information (model, operating system, app version)  
- Log data and crash reports  
- Technical identifiers necessary to ensure app stability and performance  

---

## 2. Purpose of Data Collection

Personal data is collected and used solely to:

- Authenticate users and manage accounts  
- Allow users to create, view, and manage travel itineraries  
- Enable social interactions between users (friend requests and blocking)  
- Store and manage uploaded content  
- Ensure proper functionality, stability, and security of the App  

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

- **User consent**, and  
- **Performance of the service requested by the user**

---

## 4. Data Storage and Processing

User data is securely stored and processed using the following services:

- **Firebase Authentication** – user authentication  
- **Firebase Storage** – image and file storage  
- **MongoDB** – storage of user profiles, itineraries, and social interactions  
- **Google Maps Platform** – map visualization and location selection  

Data may be processed on servers located **outside your country**, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

---

## 5. Location Data

Voyage may access location-related data **only to display maps and selected places** using Google Maps.  
The App **does not perform continuous location tracking** and does not store real-time location data unless explicitly required by a specific feature.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data **only for as long as you maintain an active account** or as necessary to provide the requested services.  
Data associated with deleted content or deleted accounts is permanently removed.

---

## 7. Data Deletion

Users can:

- Delete individual trips, permanently removing related data from the database and storage  
- Request **full account deletion**, which will remove all personal data associated with the account  

Account deletion can be requested:
- Directly from within the App (if available), or  
- By contacting us at **vinc.ingenit@gmail.com**

---

## 8. Data Sharing

User data is **not sold or shared for advertising or marketing purposes**.

Data is shared only with service providers necessary for the App’s functionality:

- Google Firebase  
- Google Maps Platform  

---

## 9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or misuse.

---

## 10. Children’s Privacy

Voyage is **not intended for children under 13** and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 11. User Rights

Users have the right to:

- Access their personal data  
- Request correction or deletion of their data  
- Withdraw consent at any time  

Requests can be made by contacting **vinc.ingenit@gmail.com**.

---

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Users will be notified of any significant changes through the App or other appropriate means.  

---

## 13. Contact Information

For questions, feedback, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy:

**Email:** vinc.ingenit@gmail.com
