## 1. Zero Tolerance Policy for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Voyage has a **strict zero-tolerance policy** regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users, particularly minors.

### 1.1 Prohibited Content

The following content is **strictly prohibited** on our platform:

- ‚ùå Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- ‚ùå Content that sexualizes minors
- ‚ùå Content that facilitates, encourages, or promotes the sexual exploitation of minors
- ‚ùå Content depicting, encouraging, or promoting child abuse
- ‚ùå Content that grooms or exploits minors
- ‚ùå Content depicting minors in a sexual or suggestive manner

### 1.2 Enforcement Actions

Any violation will result in:

1. **Immediate account suspension** without warning
2. **Permanent ban** from the platform
3. **Content removal** and evidence preservation
4. **Report to appropriate authorities** including:
   - National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
   - Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)
   - Local law enforcement agencies
   - Relevant international authorities

## 2. Age Requirements

### 2.1 Minimum Age
- Users must be **13 years or older** to use Voyage
- Parental consent required for users under 18 in jurisdictions where applicable

### 2.2 Age Verification
- Users authenticate via Google Sign-In
- Date of birth verification during account creation

## 3. Content Moderation

### 3.1 User-Generated Content
Voyage allows users to:
- Upload travel photos and memories
- Share travel itineraries
- Connect with friends

All content is subject to moderation and must comply with our policies.

### 3.2 Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior through:

1. **In-App Reporting**: Contact support within the app
2. **Email**: [vincenzo.ingenito@example.com]
3. **Emergency**: Immediate reporting to authorities

**Response Time**: All reports are reviewed within **24 hours**

### 3.3 Content Review Process

1. Automated systems scan for known CSAM using PhotoDNA technology
2. Manual review by moderation team for flagged content
3. Immediate action on policy violations
4. Regular audits of user-generated content
